About ADVP Business Ventures Pvt. Ltd.

ADVP is a leading O2O (Online-to-Offline) second landlord platform based in Bangalore. As a trusted prop tech company, we specialize in professional management of housing asset for individual apartments, villas, and houses.

Since our incorporation fromApril 2023, we have grown steadily to manage over 100+ residential real estate properties. Our platform proudly serves clients in maintaining their property and providing hassle free transactions, emphasizing our dedication to client satisfaction and building trust.

We started ADVP by identifying a fundamental gap in the Indian real estate landscape. In India, a significant portion of personal investment is tied up in property—yet the maintaining these assets is often inefficient, opaque, or prone to unethical practices.

This vision has now become our DNA, guiding every decision, service, and interaction.We are committed to realizing our vision by fostering a results-driven, positive, and energetic work environment—one that thrives on continuous development, career progression, and the cultivation of long-term, value-based relationships.
